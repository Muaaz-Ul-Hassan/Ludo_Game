Welcome to Ludo! Player 1 starts the game. Roll the dice to begin!

How to Play

  • Players take turns rolling the dice.
  • Roll a 6 to unlock a token from home and get an extra turn.
  • Move your tokens clockwise around the board.
  • Land on an opponent's token to send it back to home.
  • First player to get all 4 tokens to the center wins!

Ludo Game Rules

×

Objective

Be the first player to move all four of your tokens from your starting area, around the board, and into your home triangle.

Setup

Each player chooses a color (Red, Green, Yellow, or Blue) and places their 4 tokens in their respective starting area.

Gameplay

  • Players take turns rolling a single die.
  • To enter a token into play from its starting area, a player must roll a 6.
  • If a player rolls a 6, they get an extra turn after moving a token.
  • Tokens move clockwise around the board the number of spaces shown on the die.
  • If a token lands on a space occupied by an opponent's token, the opponent's token is sent back to its starting area.
  • Tokens on safe spaces (marked with a star) cannot be captured.
  • When a token has completed a full circuit of the board, it proceeds up the home column of its own color.

Winning

The first player to get all four of their tokens into their home triangle wins the game!
